Thank you for your interest in Little Light Montessori Based School. We invite you to explore this site and learn what makes Little Light a premier learning environment for children ages 18 months through 12.

As Dr. Maria Montessori once stated, "And so we discovered that education is not something which the teacher does, but that it is a natural process which develops spontaneously in the human being." Our primary goal is to provide a well-prepared environment that includes dedicated staff members and supportive parents who encourage and foster intrinsic motivation and independence that ultimately results in our students reaching their maximum potential.

Our educational approach is for children to trust in their own ability, to think and solve problems independently. The students learn not to be afraid of making mistakes. They learn to try and try again without fear of embarrassment. Our Montessori students are taught pride in their work and does not teach the students "what" to think but rather "how" to think. The staff guides students through the process of discovery that in turn leads to growth and increased self-confidence.

Students are known, valued and celebrated by their peers and our experienced and caring faculty. They learn at their own pace, using individual lesson plans. Challenged to achieve their personal best, Little Light graduates leave well prepared for fruitful lives, meaningful careers and responsible global citizenship.

I invite you to take the time to get to know Little Light Montessori Based School and understand the remarkable educational journey we offer to children.

We look forward to meeting you and your family soon.

Sincerely,
