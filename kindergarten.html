Kindergarten are like young scientists, eager to learn, try new things, and explore the world around them. That's why the Kindergarten Program at Little Light is more than just childcare; we provide students aged 18 months through 3 years the perfect environment to establish good habits, absorb language and become independent.

Recognizing this reality, we created safe environment that lays the foundation for all future learning. The environment is beautifully prepared for the youngest child to be independent and engaged to explore and learn by experiencing the classroom materials and interacting with a familiar group of peers. The aim of the Kindergarten environment is to aid the whole child in developing their sense of independence, gain self-confidence, care of self and care of others in the environment.

By the end of their Kindergarten experience, children are ready for the Primary environment. They are increasingly interested in learning from other children and about the world around them. The more complex social life and the vast array of materials and lessons to be learned in the Primary class satisfy their need to grow.
