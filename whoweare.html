Our Mission

Nurturing the whole child is the core of all we do. Our mission is to educate, enrich and inspire children to reach their fullest potential as kind, independent, curious and happy individuals. We make the most of the critical early learning years by inspiring a lifelong love of learning in a safe and caring environment.

Our History

The school first opened its doors in January 2012, founded by a small group of educators who were intrigued and excited by the educational methods of Dr. Maria Montessori. They believed in the basic Montessori tenet of encouraging a child’s self-realization of his/her own worth and potential. Located in Nilopool, Budhanilkantha the school began to provide kindergarten and primary program for students ages 3 to 9. It is now located nearby Nilopool, Budhanilkantha (on the way to Taulung), in newly renovated facilities, offering programs from Kindergarten (18 months - 3 years), Primary (3 - 6 years), Elementary (6 - 12 years).

Over the years, the school has come to represent the best of educational innovation with goals that are firmly rooted in a Montessori curriculum and high moral standards and continues to offer a Montessori educational experience providing excellent academics, innovative programs and a nurturing, child-centered environment.

The best way to understand the Little Light Montessori difference is to visit: I encourage you to contact us.

Little Light Montesoori Based School

At our core, we are devoted to nurturing the whole child intellectually, socially, emotionally, physically and ethically – all in a caring, safe, and fun environment.
